The Company





Desert Web Solutions was founded by John Doe in 2008. A resident of the Coachella Valley for most of his life, he found that there was a lack of web development services that could cater to the local businesses. After several years of freelance website design, and mobile application development, John decided to put together a team of engineers and establish a company location and thus, Desert Web Solutions was born.


Since then, Desert Web Solutions has been helping businesses from various parts in California to market their products/service online,
